# Managing Payments

Corridor provides a comprehensive payment platform for businesses to send and receive money globally.

## Payment Types

### Inbound Payments
Accept payments from customers through:
- **Payment Links** - Shareable links for instant payments
- **Invoices** - Professional invoices with payment tracking
- **API Integration** - Accept payments programmatically

### Outbound Payments
Send money to:
- **Employees** - Salary, bonuses, reimbursements
- **Contractors** - Project-based payments
- **Vendors** - Supplier payments
- **Mass Payouts** - Batch payments to multiple recipients

## Supported Payment Methods

| Method | Type | Speed | Fee |
|--------|------|-------|-----|
| Credit/Debit Card | Traditional | Instant | 2.9% + $0.30 |
| Bank Transfer (ACH) | Traditional | 1-3 days | 1% |
| USDC (Circle) | Stablecoin | Instant | 0.5% |
| Solana (SOL) | Crypto | Instant | Network fee only |

## Payment Flows

### Accepting Payments

1. Create a payment link or invoice
2. Share with your customer
3. Customer completes payment
4. Funds appear in your wallet instantly (for digital payments)

### Sending Payments

1. Navigate to **Payouts** or **Mass Payouts**
2. Add recipient details or import from CSV
3. Specify amount and currency
4. Review and confirm the payment
5. Track status in real-time

## Security & Compliance

All payments are:
- Encrypted end-to-end
- KYC/AML compliant
- Protected against fraud
- Fully auditable with transaction history
