# Setting Up Your Account

Get your Corridor account configured and ready to accept payments in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the [Corridor registration page](/signup)
2. Enter your business email and create a secure password
3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to **Settings > Business Profile** and provide:

- Business name and registration details
- Business address and contact information
- Tax identification number (if applicable)

## Step 3: Invite Your Team

Go to **Team** to invite team members with appropriate roles:

- **Admin** - Full access to all features
- **Finance** - Access to payments, invoicing, and financial data
- **Viewer** - Read-only access to dashboards and reports

## Step 4: Connect Your Bank

Navigate to **Finance > Add Funds** to connect your bank account:

1. Select your bank from the supported institutions
2. Follow the secure authentication flow
3. Your account will be verified within 1-2 business days

## Step 5: Enable Payment Methods

Configure which payment methods you want to accept:

- **Traditional Payments** - Credit/debit cards, bank transfers
- **Stablecoins** - USDC, USDT via Circle
- **Crypto** - Solana and other supported blockchains

## Next Steps

Now that your account is set up, learn about [Managing Payments](/docs/business/payments/overview) or explore [Invoicing](/docs/business/payments/invoicing) features.
