# Corridor Tiers and Pricing

Corridor offers a range of tiers designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals, startups, and large enterprises. Each tier provides a distinct set of features and capabilities, ensuring you have the right tools to manage your finances effectively.

## Free Tier

The Free Tier is perfect for individuals and small operations just getting started with Corridor. It allows you to explore core functionalities and begin your journey without any upfront costs.

### Features
-   Basic payment processing.
-   Limited social payment features.
-   Standard API access.
-   Access to essential EWA functionalities (for employees).

### Limitations
-   **API Rate Limit**: 100 requests per minute (as detailed in the [Authentication Guide](../api-reference/authentication.md)).
-   **Transaction Volume**: Capped at a certain monthly volume or number of transactions.
-   Limited reporting and analytics.
-   No priority support.

### Best for
Individuals, freelancers, and very small businesses.

## Pro Tier

The Pro Tier is designed for growing businesses and power users who require more robust features and higher operational capacity.

### Features
-   All Free Tier features.
-   Advanced social payment capabilities (e.g., enhanced crowdfunding, detailed contribution tracking).
-   Expanded API access with more endpoints.
-   Basic EWA integration features (e.g., CSV uploads for employee data).
-   Enhanced reporting and analytics dashboard.
-   Standard customer support.

### Limitations
-   **API Rate Limit**: 1,000 requests per minute.
-   Higher transaction volume limits.
-   Certain advanced integrations may be limited.

### Best for
Small to medium-sized businesses, agencies, and projects with moderate transaction needs.

## Premium / Enterprise Tier

The Premium/Enterprise Tier offers comprehensive solutions for large organizations, high-volume businesses, and those requiring custom integrations and dedicated support.

### Features
-   All Pro Tier features.
-   Unlimited social payment features.
-   Full API access, including custom endpoints and webhooks.
-   Advanced EWA integration (ERP/HRIS connection, full bank syncing, custom payroll cycles).
-   Real-time, customizable reporting and advanced analytics.
-   Dedicated account manager and priority 24/7 support.
-   Early access to new features.
-   Custom branding options.

### Limitations
-   Customizable based on specific needs.
-   Tier-specific rate limits are significantly higher or custom-negotiated.

### Best for
Large enterprises, financial institutions, and businesses with complex operational requirements and high transaction volumes.

## Upgrading Your Tier

You can easily upgrade your Corridor tier through your dashboard under **Settings > Billing & Plans**. For Enterprise solutions, please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.

## Clarification on Feature Availability

To ensure full transparency:
-   **Core Payment Infrastructure**: Corridor always provides the payment infrastructure. The choice of using Paystack (for deposits/withdrawals) or other external processors is based on the specific transaction type (e.g., fiat in/out vs. internal transfers) and your chosen tier.
-   **Feature Blocks**: Some advanced features, such as certain ERP integrations or very high transaction volumes, are exclusively available in Pro or Enterprise tiers. These blocks are implemented to ensure scalability, security, and the provision of dedicated resources.

We are committed to providing flexible and powerful financial tools at every level. Choose the tier that best fits your current needs, with the confidence that Corridor can scale with you.
