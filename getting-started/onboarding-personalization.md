# Personalized Onboarding and Dashboard Experience

At Corridor, we believe your financial platform should adapt to *your* needs, not the other way around. Our intelligent onboarding process is designed to understand your unique requirements and automatically tailor your dashboard and available features for a highly relevant and efficient experience.

## Your Journey Starts Here: Key Onboarding Questions

During your initial sign-up, we'll ask a few key questions to understand your primary objectives and how you plan to use Corridor. These questions are crucial for setting up your personalized workspace:

-   **What type of business are you?** (e.g., Freelancer, Small Business, Enterprise, Non-profit)
-   **What is your primary goal with Corridor?** (e.g., Accept payments, Pay employees, Manage social goals, Earned Wage Access provider)
-   **Which features are most important to you?** (e.g., Invoicing, Payroll, Crowdfunding, API Integration)

## A Dashboard Tailored Just For You

Your answers to these questions directly influence the layout and content of your Corridor dashboard:

### 1. Dynamic Dashboard Layout
-   **Relevant Widgets**: Your dashboard will prioritize widgets and data visualizations most pertinent to your stated goals. For example, if you're primarily using Corridor for EWA, you'll see EWA-specific metrics and quick actions prominently. If you're managing social goals, your dashboard will highlight goal progress and contributions.
-   **Quick Access**: Frequently used features based on your profile will be easily accessible, reducing navigation time.

### 2. Feature Visibility & Focus
-   **Clutter-Free Interface**: We only display the features and modules that are relevant to your selected use cases. If you chose "EWA provider only," your interface will be streamlined to focus solely on payroll and employee, earned wage access features. This prevents cognitive overload and allows you to focus on what matters most.
-   **Guided Setup**: For the features you've indicated interest in, the system will guide you through the necessary setup steps, making implementation straightforward.

### 3. Recommended Workflows & Integrations
-   **Smart Suggestions**: Based on your business type and goals, Corridor will suggest relevant integrations (e.g., ERP/HRIS connections for EWA providers) and automation workflows that can further enhance your operations.
-   **Resource Personalization**: Access to documentation, guides, and tutorials will be curated to match your specific needs, providing you with targeted support.

## Flexibility to Evolve

Your needs may change over time, and your Corridor experience can evolve with you. You can update your preferences and activate/deactivate features at any time through your **Settings** to re-tailor your dashboard and access new functionalities as your business grows.

Our personalized onboarding ensures that from your very first login, Corridor feels intuitive, powerful, and perfectly aligned with your financial management objectives.
